    Success Story

    Magallanes Renovables installed and tested the ATIR floating tidal platform at EMEC. The ATIR aims to leverage existing technology from the naval and wind industry to minimise the risks and focus on the unique technical challenges of tidal energy production.

    The test programme allowed Magallanes to enhance the energy production of the ATIR thanks to their remote control system, which was developed to reduce the control risk and increase security.

    Magallanes also successfully carried out a full maintenance in dry dock. This is a very complex operation, and thanks to this experience they will be able to perform the maintenance in 3 months instead of 9 months in the future.

    Next steps

    Magallanes Renovables now aims to build a full scale commercial version of their tidal platform, and to deploy their first tidal array in Morlais, North Wales.
