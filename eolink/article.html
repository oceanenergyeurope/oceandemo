    Success Story

    EOLINK 5 MW floating wind turbine is currently being manufactured and will be commissioned at SEM-REV in 2024. To prepare for this deployment, EOLINK installed 3 drag anchors and their associated bottom chain, the first step of their innovative Single Point Mooring installation.

    Ocean DEMO enabled EOLINK to finance and derisk the installation of the anchors. The operation was first of its kind and required a high degree of preparation and expertise, due to the limited depth, the space available and the proximity to other demonstrators.

    The final positions of the anchors are now fixed, allowing EOLINK to adjust the final design before installing the Single Point Mooring of their floating offshore wind turbine. These anchors have been pre-installed on the SEM-REV test site operated by the OPEN-C Foundation.

    Next steps

    EOLINK will now work towards completing the installation of their 5 MW floating wind turbine. Once deployed, the pre-commercial unit will provide energy to 7000 inhabitants.

    Multi-device plans

    The installation of the anchoring system brings EOLINK a step closer to the testing of the full prototype. Once the design is validated, bigger prototypes could be deployed, potentially in multi-device configuration.
