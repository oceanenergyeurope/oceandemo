    Success Story

    Oceans of Energy developed a unique floating offshore solar farm system. The platform has been operating at sea in rough sea conditions for over 3 years.

    Oceans of Energy’s offshore solar technology paves the way for major offshore renewable energy projects far out at sea.  With Ocean DEMO’s support, Oceans of Energy has been able to tow a large floating solar array from a nearshore site to a new site, 12km offshore.

    This towing operation has been the first long distance move of a floating solar system in the world and was executed successfully. Based on this experience, very large scale offshore solar farms of 10-1000 MW could be installed at sites far offshore and within offshore wind farms.

    Next steps

    Oceans of Energy is now looking to expand their solar platform to a larger multi-megawatt scale, fit for utility energy production.

    Multi-device plans

    Oceans of Energy’s system consists of multiple connected floating solar units that form arrays. Additional units can be added offshore to build utility scale farms.
