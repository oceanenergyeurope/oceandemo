    Success Story

    Pytheas Technology installed its innovative Power Take-Off (PTO) converting mechanical energy into electricity on a Wave Energy Converter (WEC). This system is particularly adapted to the irregularity and intermittence of wave energy generation.

    The installation of the system was carried out successfully in a very short time (2 days for installation and test of the whole PTO). The first results obtained at sea are very promising, showing a higher efficiency than traditional systems.

    Pytheas Technology’s PTO proved that it can strongly enhance the potential of wave energy converters. The system survived strong storms and produced electricity for several months on the test site.

    Next steps

    Once the performances of this first demonstrator are validated, the generator will be scaled up in power and made into a commercial industrial product.
